**SECTION 1 — OVERVIEW**

| **Purpose** | Ensure every CSM maintains clean, current data in Salesforce so that health scores, renewal forecasts, and the quarterly Book of Business review reflect reality — not stale data. |
| --- | --- |
| **Who This Is For** | All CSMs (SMB, Mid-Market, Enterprise). Non-negotiable — data hygiene is everyone's responsibility.  If you manage Alleva accounts, see the Alleva Partner Accounts SOP for Alleva-specific data hygiene requirements. |
| **Why It Matters** | Every field you update rolls up into the Master Health Score, which drives renewal forecasting, escalation decisions, and executive reporting. If the data is wrong, every decision made from it is wrong. |
| **Escalation Path** | CSM → Anna → Sarah |
| **Key Rule** | All account notes live in Chatter (SFDC) — NOT Slack. |

**ℹ** Every field you update rolls up into a picture that tells us who is at risk, who is ready to expand, and where we will land on renewals. If the data is wrong, the picture is wrong — and we make bad decisions.

**SECTION 2 — DAILY CHECKLIST (10 minutes)**

**⚠** These tasks take under 10 minutes total. Do them in the moment — don't batch them. A sentiment update logged 3 days late is a health score that was wrong for 3 days.

| **DAILY — Do these after every meaningful customer interaction** | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Task** | **SFDC Field / Location** | **Time** | **Why It Matters** |
| Update Customer Sentiment after every meaningful customer interaction | *Account → Customer\_Sentiment\_\_c* | 1 min | Sentiment score degrades to 0.01 if Unknown. Even one interaction should move it to Positive or Neutral. |
| Log Economic Buyer interactions as Events (Type = CX Economic Buyer Meeting) | *Activity → New Event → Event Type = CX Economic Buyer Meeting* | 2 min | Auto-stamps Last EB Engagement date. Score degrades automatically if >90 days — log every touchpoint. |
| Submit Risk Flag immediately for any new at-risk signal | *Risk\_Flag\_\_c → New Record → Status = Active* | 3 min | Risk Score drops to 0.10 the moment an Active flag exists. Never let a known risk sit unflagged. |
| Log all account notes in Chatter — not Slack | *Account → Chatter feed* | 2 min | Chatter is the system of record. Notes in Slack are invisible to leadership and lost in escalations. |

**SECTION 3 — WEEKLY CHECKLIST (30 minutes)**

**⚠** Block 30 minutes every week — Friday afternoon works well. Your red and yellow accounts should never sit unreviewed for more than 7 days.

| **WEEKLY — Do these every week before the team meeting** | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Task** | **SFDC Field / Location** | **Time** | **Why It Matters** |
| Review all Red accounts (Health Score < 50) — update status or escalate | *SFDC Report: At-Risk Accounts (Health Score < 65)* | 10 min | Red accounts need active intervention. Weekly review ensures nothing sits unaddressed for more than 7 days. |
| Review all Yellow accounts (Health Score 50–74) — identify which are trending down | *SFDC Report: Account Health Overview* | 10 min | Yellow accounts are on the way to red. Early intervention is cheaper than a save plan. |
| Update Meeting Cadence if your engagement frequency has changed | *Account → Meeting\_Cadence\_\_c picklist* | 2 min | Meeting Cadence Score drops significantly below Monthly. A stale cadence field understates risk. |
| Move Risk Flags from Active to Recovering when the situation is improving | *Risk\_Flag\_\_c → Status = Recovering* | 5 min | Score moves from 0.10 to 0.75 when Recovering. Reflects real progress and improves health score accurately. |
| Move Risk Flags from Recovering to Resolved when fully mitigated | *Risk\_Flag\_\_c → Status = Resolved* | 2 min | Score returns to 1.00 when no Active or Recovering flags exist. Close the loop. |
| Update Renewal Stage for any accounts that have progressed | *Renewal Object → Stage picklist* | 5 min | Stale renewal stages corrupt the weekly forecast. Affects the weighted ARR calculation leadership relies on. |

**SECTION 4 — MONTHLY CHECKLIST (60 minutes)**

**⚠** The monthly audit is your insurance policy. CSMs who do it consistently spend 90 minutes on their quarterly BoB review. CSMs who skip it spend half a day cleaning up before they can even start.

| **MONTHLY — Full book audit. Block 60 minutes.** | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Task** | **SFDC Field / Location** | **Time** | **Why It Matters** |
| Full health score audit — review all accounts in your book | *SFDC Report: Account Health Overview → filter to Current User* | 30 min | Monthly audit is the minimum to catch slow-moving declines before they become emergencies. |
| Verify CX Health Score Segment is set on every account | *Account → CX\_Health\_Score\_Segment\_\_c* | 5 min | Blank segment defaults to MM weighting. Wrong segment = wrong score. Fix it once, it stays fixed. |
| Review Product Usage scores — investigate any sharp drops | *Account → Product\_Usage\_Score\_\_c (and sub-scores)* | 15 min | Usage drops are the earliest leading indicator of churn. A 6-month decline that goes unaddressed becomes a save plan. |
| Update Renewal Forecast categories before monthly team meeting | *Renewal Object → Forecast picklist (Positive Outlook / Neutral / At Risk / Intent to Churn)* | 15 min | Leadership forecasts off this data. An account sitting in Positive Outlook when it should be At Risk inflates the forecast. |
| Ensure no accounts have Unknown Customer Sentiment | *Account → Customer\_Sentiment\_\_c — filter for Unknown* | 5 min | Unknown sentiment scores 0.01 — same as Negative. Any interaction is enough to update it. |
| Confirm at least one EB interaction logged per at-risk or yellow account | *Activity → Events → Type = CX Economic Buyer Meeting* | 10 min | EB Interaction Score hits 0.01 after 90 days of no contact. Yellow and red accounts need executive engagement. |
| Clear all resolved Risk Flags — close anything fully mitigated | *Risk\_Flag\_\_c → Status = Resolved* | 5 min | Lingering Recovering flags suppress the score to 0.75. Resolve them to return the account to 1.00. |

**SECTION 5 — PRE-QUARTERLY CHECKLIST (90 minutes — the week before your BoB review)**

**⚠** Complete this checklist in the week before your quarterly Book of Business review. Do not skip it — a 90-minute prep now saves you from presenting bad data to leadership.

| **PRE-QUARTERLY — Complete the week before your BoB review** | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Task** | **SFDC Field / Location** | **Time** | **Why It Matters** |
| Confirm zero Unknown Sentiment values across your entire book | *Account Health Overview Report → filter Sentiment = Unknown* | 10 min | Unknown values will show as 0.01 in the quarterly BoB review — embarrassing and fixable. |
| Confirm all Risk Flags are in the correct status (Active / Recovering / Resolved) | *Risk Flag Report → filter to Current User* | 15 min | Risk scores are binary — one wrong status changes a green account to red in the dashboard. |
| Verify all renewal stages are accurate for next 6-month cohort | *Renewal Stages / Pipeline Report* | 15 min | The quarterly forecast is built from this data. Wrong stages = wrong numbers in front of leadership. |
| Pull all 5 pillar score averages — fix anything that looks off before taking screenshots | *SFDC: CX Health Score Averages Report* | 20 min | Don't screenshot a dashboard with obvious data errors. Fix first, present clean. |
| Draft your themes and key asks before opening the deck | *Your notes / Chatter review* | 30 min | The BoB review is a strategic conversation, not a data readout. Know your story before you build the slides. |
| Screenshot all required SFDC dashboards and reports | *Health Score Dashboard / Renewal Forecast / At-Risk Report / Pillar Breakdown* | 20 min | Screenshots must be taken just before the review — not days in advance. Data changes. |
| Add SFDC report links to each slide in the BoB deck | *BoB Review Template → link placeholders on each slide* | 10 min | Leadership may want to drill in live. Having the links ready saves time and looks prepared. |

**SECTION 6 — QUICK REFERENCE**

**Time commitment summary:**

**•** Daily — 10 minutes (in the moment, after each interaction)

**•** Weekly — 30 minutes (block Friday afternoon)

**•** Monthly — 60 minutes (block first Monday of the month)

**•** Pre-Quarterly — 90 minutes (the week before your BoB review)

**•** Total per quarter — approximately 5-6 hours of intentional data hygiene

**The most important fields to never leave blank:**

**•** Customer Sentiment — never leave as Unknown

**•** Meeting Cadence — update whenever your cadence changes

**•** CX Health Score Segment — set once, update only if the account changes tiers

**•** Risk Flags — log immediately, never let a known risk sit unflagged

**•** EB Interaction — log as a CX Economic Buyer Meeting Event every time

**✕** All account notes live in Chatter (SFDC) — not Slack. If it is not in Chatter it does not exist.
